ROCKSTAR

Help

Click to select a song from list and then click to let the computer play it.

To play along, assign instruments to players using the green player selection bar on top of the instrument panel.

For guitars - click or touch the correct color on the control bar at the bottom of the instrument panel as the same color note reaches the bar.

For drums - stop the color pads from shrinking by repeatedly clicking/tapping the appropriate color pad.

Appropriate GH/Rockband compatible controllers are strongly recommended. I have tested various PS2, PS3 and Wii controllers and they mostly work fine.

If you have controllers you can test them for compatibility here and then assign and configure them in the settings dialog by clicking .

This app still needs lots of work so let me know if you find an issue. Have fun!
