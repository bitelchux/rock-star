Help

Click to select a song from list and then clieck to let the computer play it.

To play along, assign instruments to players using the green player selection bar on top of the instrument panel.

Click or touch the correct color on the control bar at the bottom of the instrument panel as the same color note reaches the bar.

If you have compatible controllers (test them here), assign and configure them in the settings screen .

Have fun!
